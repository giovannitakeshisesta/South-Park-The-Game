How Was Done

The idea was to make an arcade game like the old classics, Space invaders, Froggy, Snake etc.

I am a great fan of South Park , so I thought it could be entertaining developing my own South Park videogame.

The goal was to learn how to work with DOM manipulation, Object Oriented Programming, and the use of event listeners.
